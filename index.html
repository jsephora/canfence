About Can Fence

Can Fence is a local company that is dedicated to looking after the local community.

We can repair your old fence or build you a brand new one. We’re Canberra’s custom fence specialist.

We’ll look after all your fencing needs and provide top class service, excellent pricing and second to none workmanship.

All our work is guaranteed and we only use the highest quality materials.

Services

We specialize in Colorbond and timber fencing, fence maintenance, repairs and removals, gates and security. We offer competitive rates and discounts for seniors. No job too big or small.


Fence repairs and maintenance

Your old fence may have good bones, but just needs some TLC.

We can do minor repairs or give your fence a full refresh. We'll even remove and dispose of any unwanted materials for you.

New Colorbond or timber fences supplied and installed

Colorbond fences come in a range of colours, are durable and create instant privacy.

Timber fences add character and charm and can be painted in any colour you like. They are highly customisable.

Security gates and fences

Ensure your family's safety with secure gates and fences.

We install pool fences, child-proof gates and gateway security.
